Our Goals

Our Goal

Here at Hilltop Music Academy (H.M.A), We believe worship is our reason for existence and so are you. Our goal has been and will continue to be
"Raising a new breed of worshipers with relevance"


Our Classes

Our Classes

Our classes run 2 days a week and each class runs from 4pm to 6pm, where we take vocalist on one and instrumentalist on another. We have four different levels of classes that run for 12 weeks.


Scope

Our Scope

We are open to everyone. Student are permitted to sign up for both classes depending of their availability.
We reqiure no prior experience
