WHAT IS HILTOP MUSIC ACADEMY

This a Christian based institution, committed to empower aspiring music ministers and worshippers with the skills needed to survive in the music art ministry and what it takes to offer a Kingdom based kind of worship. Our goal is to impact and transform the lives of our students with worship and music

WHAT MATTERS TO US MOST

Being a skilled and talented musician is a very important aspect in worship but that’s not enough. we take you through an in-depth understanding of what it takes to offer a kind of worship that is in spirit and in truth (John4:24), which is the kind of worship that creation is waiting for (Rom.8:19), if worship must be done appropriately , it must be done from a place of understanding and with a conscious mind .

WHAT IS OUR GOAL

Here at Hilltop Music Academy (H.M.A), we believe worship is our reason for existence and so are you. Our goal has been and will continue to be “RAISING A NEW BREED OF WORSHIPPERS WITH RELEVANTS”. We are committed to ensure that God’s plan and purpose for your life is achieved and above all spreading the life of the Kingdom.

WHO IS A CANDIDATE OF H.M.A

  • A vocalist and or instrumentalist who wants to glorify God with his/her voice or instrument
  • A worship leader who wants to acquire more skills in order to transform the praise and worship in their congregation
  • Anyone interested in becoming a seasoned musician
  • Anyone passionate about music and Kingdom worship life.

OUR CLASSES

H.M.A is here to provide you with the right tools and resources you need. Our classes are designed to help you grow spiritually but also designed to help develop you in all aspects of music, challenging you to grow in your music ministry and also advances you in all aspects of life so you can be relevant in the 21st century music ministry work and dominate in the music industry.
Our classes runs 4 hours a week in 2 days. From 4pm till 6pm, where we take vocalist on one and instrumentalist on another. We have four different levels of classes that runs for 12 weeks. Student are permitted to sign up for both classes depending of their availability.
N.B: NO EXPERIENCE IS REQUIRED

For Instrumentalist

Beginers

  • Basic music theory
  • Music accompaniment
  • Rythm
  • Chord formation
  • Chord progression
  • Basic chord substitution
  • Intro to drop 2 and more

Intermediate

  • Accompaniment
  • Chords and progresions
  • Drop 2s
  • Different Praise and worship yythms
  • Reharmonization and more

Advanced

  • Advanced Chord formation and progresions
  • Deeper Look at praise and worship atmospherem, Music, Man creation and God
  • Advanced song writing and more

For Singers

Beginers

  • Ear training and development
  • Rhythm
  • Breathing technique
  • Basic music theory
  • An auxiliary instrument and more

Intermediate

  • Harmony
  • Background Singers and Techniques
  • Vocal Techniques
  • Leading praise and worship
  • Song writing techniques and more

Advanced

  • Advanced Vocal techniques
  • Deeper Look at praise and worship atmospherem, Music, Man creation and God
  • Advanced song writing and more
